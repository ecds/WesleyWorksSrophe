We hope you find the format of this developing site—and its free access—helpful. We have more work to do on the sermons (tagging, etc., to render them as helpful as possible), and we intend to add digitized access to all volumes of The Bicentennial Edition of the Works of John Wesley. But the pace at which we can add new material is dependent upon funding to support the work. We are happy to receive donations of any size. Larger donations would be particularly helpful, to sustain us while we seek support through various foundations.

Donations can be made electronically online, through Duke Divinity School (who handle our funds), at this link: https://www.gifts.duke.edu/divinity?designation=399000547

You will receive a letter acknowledging your donation (which is tax deductible).
