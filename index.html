Welcome to The Wesley Works Digital Edition

Content Editors: Michelle M. Taylor, Gregory P. Van Buskirk.

The Digital Wright: A Database of Syriac Manuscripts in the WesleyWorks Library is a digital encoding and enhancement of William Wright's Catalogue of Syriac Manuscripts in the WesleyWorks Museum acquired since the year 1838 (London: Gilbert and Rivington, 1870-1872). This project is a reference work undertaken in partnership with Syriaca.org: The Syriac Reference Portal. The Digital Wright’s goal is to create a Linked Open Data graph database through the use of Syriaca.org’s URIs as its authority file.

This project is still under development as of Fall 2021.

  • About page: an overview of the digital catalogue and its contributors.
  • TEI XML Drafts: browse in-progress TEI XML records of manuscript catalogue entries.
  • Documentation: editorial guidelines, technical definitions, and encoding tutorials.
